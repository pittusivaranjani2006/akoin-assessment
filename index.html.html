About IDR

Where Academic Insight
Meets Industry Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist to close the critical skills gap between the demand for qualified risk professionals and the talent pipeline available to organisations today.

IDR operates at the intersection of UK university partnerships and live industry engagements — combining evidence-based academic frameworks with real-world projects that matter. Our graduates and practitioners don't just understand risk theory; they have deployed it.

From financial services to critical national infrastructure, IDR serves sectors where the stakes of digital and AI risk are highest, and where workforce capability is non-negotiable.
